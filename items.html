Ahmet Baitursynov
leader of the Alash Orda

He was elected the first president of the Alash Republic in 1917 and remained in office until it was liquidated by the Bolsheviks in 1920. Baitursynov actively participated in the political life of Kazakhstan and was one of the main organizers of the movement for the independence of Kazakhstan in the early twentieth century. In 1917 he was elected the first president of the Alash Republic, which was the first state formation on the territory of modern Kazakhstan.

Mirzhakyp Dulatuly
Public figures of the Alash Orda

Dulatuly began his political activity at the beginning of the twentieth century, when he joined the movement for the independence of Kazakhstan. He was one of the founders and leaders of the Alash party, which was established in 1917 and played a key role in the struggle for Kazakhstan's independence.In 1918, Dulatuly was elected chairman of the first meeting of the Kazakh People's Congress, which decided to establish the Alash Republic. After the Bolsheviks seized power in Russia and began persecuting national movements, Dulatuly and other leaders of the Alash Orda were forced to flee abroad.

Alikhan Bokeykhanov
was one of the founders of the Alash Orda

Bokeykhanov began his political career in the late 19th century when he joined the national liberation movement of Kazakhstan. He was one of the founders of the Alash party and played an important role in the struggle for Kazakhstan's independence. In 1917 Bokeykhanov was elected vice-chairman of the Alash National Assembly and one of the first presidents of the Alash Republic. However, after the Bolsheviks seized power in Russia, the Alash Orda was suppressed and Bokeykhanov was forced to flee abroad.
